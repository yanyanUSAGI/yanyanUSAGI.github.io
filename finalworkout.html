HOURGLASS

Description:

This workout is designed to shape and define the waist, hips, and shoulders, helping you achieve an hourglass figure. It combines targeted strength exercises with a mix of core and lower body moves to enhance curves while building a strong foundation.

Goals:

To tone the waist, build rounded shoulders, and shape the hips and glutes for a balanced, hourglass look.

SHRED & TONE

Description:

A high-energy routine focused on burning fat and sculpting lean muscle. This workout combines cardio bursts with toning exercises for the whole body, making it ideal for those looking to shed extra pounds and reveal definition.

Goals:

To increase calorie burn, improve endurance, and tone muscles for a lean, defined physique.

ABS

Description:

A core-focused workout that targets the abdominal muscles with a combination of crunches, planks, leg raises, and rotational moves. This routine helps to strengthen the core, support posture, and define the midsection.

Goals:

To build core strength, improve stability, and define the abdominal muscles for a toned appearance.

GLUTES

Description:

This glute-focused workout incorporates squats, lunges, and leg lifts to target and strengthen the muscles of the hips and glutes. The routine helps to lift, tone, and sculpt the backside.

Goals:

To build glute strength, enhance muscle tone, and achieve a round, lifted shape.

FLEXIBILITY

Description:

This workout is designed to improve flexibility and mobility through stretching and yoga-inspired moves. It targets major muscle groups to enhance range of motion, reduce stiffness, and prevent injuries.

Goals:

To increase flexibility, improve joint mobility, and support muscle recovery.
