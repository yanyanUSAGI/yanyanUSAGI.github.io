Mindfulness and Meditation

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Method

This exercise helps calm your mind by focusing on your surroundings. Take a deep breath and notice:
  • 5 things you can see around you.
  • 4 things you can feel, like the texture of your clothes or the surface beneath you.
  • 3 things you can hear, whether they’re nearby sounds or in the distance.
  • 2 things you can smell, like fresh air or a nearby scent.
  • 1 thing you can taste, even if it’s just the hint of your last meal or a sip of water.
This grounding exercise is a quick way to bring yourself back to the present and find calm.
