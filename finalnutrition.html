This Week's Recipe Line Up

Yogurt Parfait | Chicken Salad | Steak-Frites | Banana Chia Pudding

Breakfast: Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt (plain or flavored as desired)
  • 1/2 cup granola (low sugar, if preferred)
  • 1/2 cup fresh berries (e.g., strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds or ground flaxseeds (for extra fiber and nutrients)
  • A handful of nuts (almonds, walnuts, or pecans) for added crunch (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by spooning a portion of Greek yogurt into a bowl or jar.
  2. Sprinkle granola over the yogurt layer for crunch.
  3. Place a layer of fresh berries on top of the granola for natural sweetness and flavor.
  4. Repeat the yogurt, granola, and berry layers until you fill the jar.
  5. Drizzle with honey or maple syrup, sprinkle chia seeds or flaxseeds, and add a few nuts on top if desired.
  6. Enjoy immediately, or refrigerate for up to 24 hours for a quick grab-and-go breakfast.

Lunch: Classic Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked, shredded or diced chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt (optional, for a lighter version)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped grapes or dried cranberries
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley or dill (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lettuce leaves or whole-grain bread, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mayonnaise and Greek yogurt until smooth.
  2. Add the chicken, celery, red onion, and grapes or cranberries to the bowl, and mix well.
  3. Season the salad with salt and pepper to taste, and add fresh herbs if desired.
  4. Serve the chicken salad on lettuce leaves or as a filling for whole-grain bread for a tasty sandwich.
  5. Enjoy immediately, or refrigerate for up to 3 days for a quick lunch option.

Dinner: Steak Frites

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks (1 inch thick)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • Fresh rosemary and thyme sprigs (optional)
  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Sea salt, for seasoning fries

Instructions

  1. Season the steaks generously with salt and black pepper on both sides.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering. Add the steaks and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or adjust to your preferred doneness.
  3. During the last minute of cooking, add butter, garlic, rosemary, and thyme to the skillet. Baste the steaks with the melted butter using a spoon.
  4. Remove the steaks from the skillet and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
  5. Peel and cut the potatoes into thin fries. Rinse them in cold water to remove excess starch, then pat them dry.
  6. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot to 350°F (175°C). Fry the potatoes in batches until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels and season with sea salt.
  7. Serve the steaks with a side of crispy fries for a delicious dinner.

Dessert: Banana Chia Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup almond milk (or milk of choice)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1-2 teaspoons honey or maple syrup (optional, for sweetness)
  • Fresh fruit or nuts for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mash the banana until smooth.
  2. Add the chia seeds, almond milk, vanilla extract, and sweetener (if using) to the bowl. Stir well to combine.
  3. Cover the mixture and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight, to allow the chia seeds to absorb the liquid and form a pudding-like texture.
  4. Stir the pudding again before serving, and add a little more almond milk if it's too thick.
  5. Top with fresh fruit, nuts, or other toppings of your choice.
  6. Enjoy your healthy and delicious banana chia pudding!
