

<p>
Oh, the cat, <br> 
with eyes so sly, <br> 
A master of mischief, oh my, oh my! 
</p>
<p>
Climbing curtains, 
scratching chairs, <br>
Leaving fur just everywhere. 
</p>
<p>
At midnight, they begin their race, <br>
Zooming around with lightning pace. <br>
Knocking over every vase, <br>
With a smug look on their face.
</p>
<p>
They steal your spot, <br>
they steal your food, <br>
Always in a mischievous mood. <br>
</p>
<p>
But despite their naughty ways, We love them more with each passing day.
